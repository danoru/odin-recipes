Color and Light

Inspired by Sunday in the Park with George (1983)

Description

What could be better for a hot, summer's day than a sundae in the park with George?

    Ingredients

  • 3 scoops of vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup of strawberries, diced with stems removed
  • 2 tablespoons of chocolate ganache
  • 1/4 cup of whipped cream

    Steps

  1. Add ice cream to your bowl.
  2. Top the ice cream with one third of your strawberries.
  3. Top the ice cream with the second third of your strawberries.
  4. Top the ice cream with the last third of your strawberries.
  5. Dot the ice cream with your ganache.
  6. Pipe your whipped cream onto your ice cream.
  7. Continue posing for your friend George's painting.
