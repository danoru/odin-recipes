A Little Priest

Inspired by Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street (1979)

Description

    Ingredients

  • 1 medium potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1 pound ground meat
  • 1/3 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 15 ounce refrigerated pie crust

    Steps

  1. Go for a shave and a haircut at your local barber.
  2. Procure your choice of meat.
  3. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  4. Boil your potato, mash when done.
  5. Crumble your meat and combine with garlic and onion in a cast iron skillet. Saute until done.
  6. Cover your skillet with the pie crust and bake.
  7. Throw the whole pie away and go grab some takeout instead.
