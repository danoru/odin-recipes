My Shot

Inspired by Hamilton (2015)

Description

You don't want to throw away your shot, so gather your closest pals and raise a glass!

    Ingredients

  • 1 oz Caribbean rum
  • 1 oz extremely verbose monologue, rapped

    Steps

  1. Pour the rum into a large shot glass.
  2. Raise up your glass.
  3. Tell your comrades how great you all are (in song).
  4. Do not throw away your shot.
